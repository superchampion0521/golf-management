WHAT IS GEORGE'S METHOD?

The beauty in George's methodology is that he really doesn't have one. Rather, George has developed a seemless and dynamic approach to crea- ting match ups for every skill level and physical make up. This coaching process consists of four things. 1. creating efficient movement. 2. develop- ing speed and power 3. allowing statistics to drive practice. 4. building complete players that are strong in all areas of the game.
